## Tips for Choosing the Perfect D&D Gift

- **Match the gift to their role** - Dungeon Masters and players have different needs and preferences
- **Consider their experience level** - Beginners might appreciate starter materials, while veterans might prefer premium accessories
- **Think about their campaign style** - Some players prefer combat-heavy games, while others focus on storytelling and roleplay
- **Factor in their gaming group** - Consider gifts that can be shared or enhance group experiences
- **Personalize when possible** - Items with their character's name or custom designs add special meaning
