## Tips for Choosing the Perfect Pet Gift

- **Consider the pet's species and size** - Different pets have different needs and preferences
- **Think about practical needs vs. entertainment** - Balance fun items with useful accessories
- **Personalize when possible** - Items with their pet's name or photo add special meaning
- **Factor in their lifestyle** - Active pet owners might prefer practical items, while others might appreciate decorative pieces
- **Consider their home environment** - Choose items that fit well with their living space and decor
